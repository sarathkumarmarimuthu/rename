Job 3: Alphabetic Filing Rules 9-10
Overview of Jobs 1-4

Auric Systems, Inc. maintains a customer information database for our cell phone users, as well as a correspondence file for paper documents.

As records manager for Auric Systems, Inc., your duties include filing in alphabetic order name cards for the cell phone customers. In the first four jobs of this simulation, you will apply the alphabetic indexing rules 1-10, using cards bearing the names and addresses of cell phone customers.

Applying the alphabetic indexing rules to cards requires attention to detail and following correct procedures. The latter is accomplished with the aid of a set of uniform indexing rules. The rules are available for your review by clicking the "Rules" tab at the bottom of your screen. You will gradually commit the rules to memory as you work with them.

Instructions for each job include a statement concerning the nature of work to be done and
step-by-step procedures to be followed. Giving careful attention to detail and systematically following the directions step by step will simplify your work.

After you have completed each job, check your work carefully. When you have completed an assignment requiring the alphabetic rearrangement of a group of names, be prepared to explain to your instructor the order of your arrangement.

Job 3: Alphabetic Filing Rules 9-10
Directions for Job 3

In this job, you will be filing in alphabetic and numeric order customer cards for the Broadband Communications Division of Auric Systems, Inc., by applying alphabetic filing Rules 9-10 to a series of 18 cards. For each card, you will:

  • Apply proper indexing and coding.
  • Identify and complete necessary cross references.
  • Sort and file cards in the correct order.

A video demonstration will help you get started. Card hints are available along the way.

Information

Sorting

Place each of the cards into the correct alphabetical or numerical sequence by dragging and dropping them into the correct order from left to right. To move a card into a different letter, drag and drop the card to the correct letter. You may also move to a different letter file, by clicking on the letter.

Coding

Code: Step 1: Separate the filing segment into units by inserting a diagonal slash (/) between the units.
To remove a slash that has already been placed, click on the slash.

Code: Step 2(Units): Indicate the primary unit (key unit) by clicking on it to underline it.
Then, click on the remaining units in sequence to place the correct number above each identified unit.

Cross-reference: Determine what should be placed on the cross-reference card by typing in the correct information.
After you have filed the cross-reference card(s), you will be able to file the main card.
